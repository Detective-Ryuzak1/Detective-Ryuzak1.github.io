Mainstream is a graffiti handwriting font, designed created by Måns Grebäck. 
It is vivid, artistic and contains a wide range of characters. 

Write underscores after any word to make an underline. 
Example: Mainstream_

Write * after any letter to put a crown on it. 
Example: Mainstre*am
